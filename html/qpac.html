Landmark in Brisbane

Located in south Brisbane area, two beautiful cultural gems are decorating Brisbane River.

Queensland Performing Arts Centre

Queensland Performing Arts Centre

The QPAC is located in Southbank, a part of the Cultural Centre. The structure is built in the mid-1970 by local architect Robin Gibson. QPAC is not only a place display performance such as musical, operas, and ballets, it also is a historical landmark of Brisbane. The Brisbane River is next to the QPAC and Qeii Park. The famous Brisbane logo is right beside QPAC. QPAC is definitely a place good for visiting and resting.
