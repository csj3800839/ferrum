Queensland Museum

Queensland Museum is the Queensland state museum, which is right next to the Queensland Art Gallery and Gallery of Modern art. The museum is moved to the current location in 1986, which makes it a part of the cultural centre of Brisbane along with the State Library of Queensland, the Queensland, Queensland Art Gallery, the Gallery of Modern Art, and the Queensland Performing Arts Centre. As one of the main parts of the cultural centre, ignore the distinct collection Museum had, and the frequent operating art or science exhibits, the outlook of the museum is also a huge attraction for the tourist and it is great to rest your foot around.
