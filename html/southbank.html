Traffic in Brisbane

Even normal dull traffic in Brisbane is different, the grey concrete road is transformed into huge canvases for artists to experiment on.

Southbank station

Southbank station

Southbank station is also a traffic transmit spot filled with stylish wall art and mural designs. The definition of Southbank station is way larger than the Indooroopilly railway station, which both contain a huge bus station and railway station in a close distance. The way between two traffic station is covered with wall art and murals, and in the way between them are the Queensland Performing Arts Centre (QPAC) and Queensland Museum, Both are the landmark of Brisbane.
